Strategic improvement actions

What Sportetitive should improve next

The organisation should move beyond immediate recovery and build a stronger resilience model. The recommendations below focus on the weaknesses exposed by the incident and the governance improvements needed to reduce repeat risk.

Strengthen identity security

Implement MFA across email, cloud, VPN, privileged accounts, backups and supplier access. Separate admin accounts, reduce standing privilege and review access rights regularly.

Improve supplier governance

Require stronger supplier access controls, clearer contractual obligations, monitoring, incident notification duties and regular assurance reviews for critical third parties.

Address the legacy POS risk

Treat the POS platform as a board-level risk. Use compensating controls in the short term and build a replacement or redesign roadmap in the medium term.

Increase monitoring and detection

Expand logging, alerting and endpoint visibility so unusual logins, privilege changes, tool disablement and suspicious data movement can be identified earlier.

Strengthen backup resilience

Maintain segregated, tested and trusted backups, supported by documented recovery priorities and regular restore exercises.

Improve governance and exercises

Run tabletop exercises, board reviews and lessons-learned sessions regularly. Cyber risk should be visible on the risk register and tracked through meaningful board metrics.
